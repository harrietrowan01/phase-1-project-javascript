The Water Divination Test


Nen users classify their aura into six categories: Enhancement, Transmutation, Conjuration, Specialization, Manipulation, and Emission. To figure out the type of aura you have, you can use the water test. The reaction of the water will tell you which type of aura you pocess.

  • Enhancers will make the water overflow.
  • Transmuters will change the taste of the water.
  • Conjurers will cause impurities to appear in the water.
  • Manipulators will cause the leaf to move on the water's surface.
  • Emitters will change the color of the water.
  • If any other change to the water appears, then the user is a Specialist.
